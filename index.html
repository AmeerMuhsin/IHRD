Welcome to College of Applied Science

The College of Applied Science Vazhakkad Managed by IHRD,.... a Govt. of Kerala undertaking is affiliated to the University of Calicut. The college offers UG programmers such as B.Sc Computer Science, BCA and B.Com with Computer Application. The UG courses are conducted as per the regulations of FYUGP-2024 of the University of Calicut. The courses follow most modern syllabi revised from time to time by the University.
